WELCOME TO APC COLLEGE

We started spirit since the year 2007 in a Hostel building at Pratapgarh and in the session 2008-09, we Placed in Fully modernized Campus to provide Quality Education for success with well-furnished classroom, technical lab with modern gadgets, well-equipped Science laboratories well-managed library, internet facility, conference hall, spacious playground, extra-curricular facilities etc. The campus has been planned as per international standards with the focus on national/international collaborations. This building has most of the features of a green building like – water harvesting, water recycling, solar electricity, to name a few. We have moved a step ahead in our endeavors to earn excellence in the dynamic global academic scenario. APC is the first college of Pratapgarh District has got Permanent No Objection Certificate from Higher Education Department Of Rajasthan & now APC go for NAAC accreditation and it speaks of our commitment to quality education.
